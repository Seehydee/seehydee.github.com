Services

Main Services I Offer:

There are many variations to the services I offer and some that require special request, but these four (4) are the hallmark of the services I offer.


If you need something different, be sure to reach out and describe your requirement(s). Thank you.

Store Migration

Have your store on some other platform like Wix, Webflow, or Squarespace and would like to take advantage of the range of solutions Shopify offers? I'll migrate your complete store data (products, orders, customers, etc). I'll also build your current theme for the Shopify platform if you would like to keep it.

Shopify Theme Customization

Need new design elements or want to change certain aspects of your current design? This service solves that! You provide me with your specifications or mockup (if you have one designed) and I build it out for you.

Shopify Custom Theme Development

Want to stand out and don't want to use any of the already available themes? I offer design and development services for custom theme development. If you a design ready, then it goes even faster as I can directly start building your custom theme!

Development of Custom Feature

Need a functionality that didn't come with Shopify or the theme you currently use? I'll build the feature for you, fully functional, budget-friendly, and without bugs!

About Me

The Front-end Developer You've Been Looking For!

I've been building websites and developing custom solutions for Shopify merchants since 2017. During this time, I've worked with over seventy (70) merchants bringing their ideas to life and giving them the tools they need to build a successful online presense.

What I offer my clients is efficiency and full functionality. Every single line of code is written with this in mind to ensure that my clients have no issues with their storefronts once I've handed over the reigns.
